# User Guide

Welcome to Stoicpips. This guide will help you navigate the platform and get your automated trading bot running efficiently.

## 1. Getting Started

### Account Registration
- Navigate to [stoicpips.com/signup](https://stoicpips.com/signup).
- Enter your email and create a secure password.
- Verify your email address to activate your account.

### Integration with Deriv
- Before starting the bot, ensure you have a valid **Deriv API Token**.
- Go to your **Settings** page in the Stoicpips dashboard.
- Paste your API Token into the "Deriv Token" field and save.

## 2. Configuring Your Bot

The bot's performance depends on the parameters you set. You can find these settings in the **Bot Configuration** panel on your dashboard.

### Trading Symbols
Select from a variety of Synthetic Indices and Forex pairs:
- **Volatility 100 (1s) Index** (R_100)
- **Volatility 50 Index** (R_50)
- **Jump Indices**, etc.

### Trade Parameters
- **Stake Amount**: The amount of capital to risk per trade.
- **Timeframe**: The candle interval the bot analyzes (e.g., 1m, 5m, 1h).
- **Strategy Mode**: Choose between "Scalping" (high frequency) or "Day Trading" (low frequency).

## 3. Risk Management

Stoicpips prioritizes capital preservation. Configure these limits to protect your balance:

- **Daily Stop Loss**: The maximum amount you're willing to lose in a single day. The bot will automatically stop if this limit is hit.
- **Max Active Trades**: Number of trades the bot can have open simultaneously.
- **Take Profit (TP) / Stop Loss (SL)**: Set fixed ratios or allow the "Stoic" strategy to handle exits dynamically based on Market Zones.

## 4. Monitoring Your Trades

The **Dashboard** provides real-time monitoring:
- **Active Trades**: View entry price, current price, and live P&L.
- **Trade History**: A detailed log of all executed trades and their outcome.
- **Performance Charts**: Visualize your profit growth and win rate over time.

## 5. Subscription Tiers

- **Free Tier**: Limited stake amounts ($10 max) and basic strategies.
- **Pro Tier**: Unlimited stake sizes, advanced strategy access, and priority signal generation.
