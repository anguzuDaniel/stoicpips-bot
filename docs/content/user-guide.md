# User Guide

Welcome to Dunam AI. Experience the power of **Zero Emotion** trading. This guide provides comprehensive instructions on setting up your account, navigating the platform, and optimizing your trading bot.

## 1. Navigating the Platform

The sidebar is your central hub for moving between the different sections of the application:

- **Dashboard**: Monitor live operations, start/stop the bot, and view real-time market scanning logs.
- **Account Profile**: Manage your personal information and view your subscription tier.
- **Bot Settings**: Configure your Deriv integration and technical trading parameters.
- **Pricing / Upgrade**: View different subscription tiers and upgrade your account.
- **Trade History**: A searchable and filterable log of every trade executed by the bot.
- **Analytics**: Deep-dive into your profit charts, success rates, and trading pair efficiency.

## 2. Setting Up Your Account

### Initial Onboarding & 7-Day Trial
New users can explore the dashboard without immediate commitment.
1. **Start Trial**: Click the **"Start Bot"** button on the Dashboard for the first time.
2. **Accept**: A "Start Your 7-Day Free Trial" card will appear. Click **"Start My Journey"** to activate full access.
3. **Monitor**: Once activated, a countdown badge will appear in the sidebar showing your remaining trial days.

### Integration with Deriv
To execute real or demo trades, you must connect your Deriv account:
1. Obtain an **API Token** from your Deriv account settings (ensure "Trading" and "Read" permissions are checked).
2. Navigate to **Bot Settings** in Dunam AI.
3. Paste your token into the **"Deriv Token"** field.
4. Set your **App ID** (default is provided, but custom IDs are supported).
5. Click **"Save Settings"**.

## 3. Operating the Trading Bot

### Starting the Engine
- Go to the **Dashboard**.
- Ensure your "Account Type" (Real or Demo) is toggled correctly in the top right.
- Click **"Start Engine"**.
- Monitor the **Scanning Markets** log to see the AI analyze RSI and Market Zones in real-time.

### Understanding Analytics
Navigate to the **Analytics** page to evaluate your strategy:
- **Apex Performers**: Identifies which trading pairs are yielding the highest ROI.
- **Underperformers**: Highlights pairs that may need tighter risk management or exclusion.
- **Stoic Insight**: "Discipline in loss is as vital as discipline in profit."

## 4. Expiry & Maintenance

### Trial Expiration
Once your 7-day trial concludes:
- The dashboard will be locked.
- You will be redirected to the **Trial Expired** page.
- Select "Upgrade to Pro" to continue your journey and unlock unlimited trading capacity.

### Troubleshooting
- **401 Unauthorized**: Your Deriv token may have expired or lacks permissions. Refresh it in Settings.
- **400 Bad Request**: Ensure your "Stake Amount" and "Stop Loss" adhere to your account's balance limits.
